The Great Gatsby is a novel by F. Scott Fitzgerald, first published in 1925. The story takes place in the summer of 1922 in the fictional town of West Egg on Long Island. The main character, Jay Gatsby, is a wealthy young man known for throwing extravagant parties. He becomes obsessed with his former love interest, Daisy Buchanan, and tries to win her back despite her being married to another man.The novel is a critique of the American Dream and the corruption and decadence of the wealthy elite in the 1920s. It is considered a classic of modernist literature and is regularly studied in schools and universities.
