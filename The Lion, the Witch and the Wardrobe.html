"The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe" is a fantasy novel written by C.S. Lewis, first published in 1950. It is the first book in "The Chronicles of Narnia" series, which consists of seven books in total. The novel tells the story of four siblings - Peter, Susan, Edmund, and Lucy - who discover a magical wardrobe that leads them to a land called Narnia, where they embark on a quest to defeat the evil White Witch and restore the rightful ruler, Aslan the lion, to his throne.
